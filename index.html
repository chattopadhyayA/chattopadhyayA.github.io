About this page

I am a theoretical physicist interested in various aspects of theoretical physics mainly in string theory and related topics. I have completed my bachelors and masters in physics from Visva-Bharati and moved to IISER Bhopal to pursue PhD in theoretical physics. After finishing my degree in 2019 I have done postdoctoral fellowships in IMSc Chennai and WITS University.

Currently I am a C2W postdoctoral fellow at the University of Mons in Belgium. Beyond academia, I am a coding enthusiast and an aspiring musician trying to learn string instruments. This webpage is designed to be the nest of my varied interests and thoughts in different aspects of life. This webpage is a work-in-progress and any suggestion is welcome.
